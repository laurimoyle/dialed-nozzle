Privacy Policy — Dialed Nozzle

Last updated: 5/29/2026

Dialed Nozzle ("the app") is a tool for 3D printing and 3D pen settings, compatibility, troubleshooting, and personal spool tracking. This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

Information we collect

None. Dialed Nozzle does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts and no sign-up.

Data you enter (spool log)

Any information you add — such as filament brands, colours, temperatures, weights, ratings, and notes — is stored only on your own device. It is never sent to us or to any third party. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information is removed from your device and cannot be recovered by us, because we never had it.

Internet access

The app is designed to work fully offline. It may load a web font and its own files the first time it runs, after which it functions without a connection. It does not track your usage, location, or behaviour.

Analytics and advertising

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking software.

Purchases

If you choose to unlock optional features through a one-time in-app purchase, that transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details. Please see Google's own privacy policy for how Google processes purchases.

Children

The app is a general-purpose utility and is suitable for all ages. Because it collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date above.
